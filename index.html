About

R.E.D. strives to offer useful, unique, appealing, and musical audio products.

Growing up I quickly became fascinated with many forms of art and technology. I somehow knew at an early age, maybe about 7 or 8, that I was going to be some kind of scientist. At that time as any boy of that age, I had an interest in the cosmos, dinosaurs, and played with chemicals and microscopes. I also picked up an interest in drawing and sketching quite early on but didn't know that I would very soon (in a short number of years, at about 14) would develop a love of guitar and music. As those young years do, they flew on by despite my belief to the contrary at that time. I soon made it through highschool and made the quick decision to chase audio engineering in college. From there I gained an interest, rightly so, in the very technology used in music production and recording for many years. This sparked the desire in me to have my own business one day making and repairing audio equipment. Without any further suspense, here we are! You and I together on the gateway to your future in new pallettes of musical audio and of course... the result of many years of growing aspirations on my part.

My name is Calvin Wayne Robinson,
and I welcome you to Robinson Electronics Divisions

"Ascension"

One of a kind boost pedal intended to drive the front end of your amplifier providing more versatility in gain structures for rhythm, soloing, or just adding a bolder touch to your playing. A single and simple "Boost" control provides a large range of adjustment while the "Focus" control can allow for a reasonably transparent tone when off (down), or deliver a touch more gain when on (up) along with a tighter, more defined low end and a moderate bell boosted at 4KHz The boost pedal has always been a staple of many player's boards around the world. It is a simple but effective tool to help get the most out of your setup or even add a little magic that wasn't there before!
